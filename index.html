Gross / Net Income calculator

Easy and fun way to calculate your net pay or "take-home-money" based on the Wage Tax System of Macedonia.

OUR APP

Calculate your net pay or "take-home-money", which remains after deducting all taxes and contributions. The calculator is based on the Wage Tax System of Macedonia. In addition to calculating what the net amount resulting from a gross amount is, our gross/net calculator can also calculate the gross wage that would yield a specific net amount. In other words, employees wishing to have a specific net salary in a month can use this tool to calculate how high the gross salary would have to be to yield the desired net amount.

FEATURES

  • Calculate your monthly net pay by entering gross amount
  • Calculate your monthly gross amount by entering monthly net pay
  • Convert amount in different currencies
  • Tax calculation by specific year
  • Visual representation of the calculations
  • Share calculation results
